How to read the quadrants

🔴 High Automation + High Reskilling

Strong displacement pressure but real capacity to respond. Priority for structured reskilling programmes. Examples: Administration, Technical.

🟠 High Automation + Low Reskilling

Highest risk zone. Strong pressure with limited structural capacity to adapt. Requires policy-level intervention. Examples: Manufacturing (emerging markets).

🟢 Low Automation + High Reskilling

Stable sector with high adaptive capacity. Proactive upskilling opportunity — these roles will evolve, not disappear. Examples: Knowledge, Management.

⚪ Low Automation + Low Reskilling

Limited immediate risk but may warrant monitoring. Often found in emerging markets where both AI adoption and education are lower.
