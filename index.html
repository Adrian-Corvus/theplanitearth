How accurate is the analysis?

The scores are grounded in published labour market and AI capability data (WEF, O*NET, McKinsey, Stanford AI Index). Your quiz answers or CV refine the baseline. Like any forward-looking model, it reflects the best available evidence — not a crystal ball. We show you the data sources so you can verify.

What do you do with my CV or LinkedIn data?

Your data is used solely to generate your report. We do not store your CV after processing, sell your data, or use it to train AI models. Your LinkedIn URL is only used to read your public profile. We take privacy seriously — see our privacy policy for full details.

How is this different from a generic AI risk score?

Most "AI risk scores" just look at your job title and spit out a number. We analyse your actual task composition, sector, experience level, and role context. Two people with the same job title can have very different risk profiles — and your brief reflects that.

What exactly is in the Career Intelligence Brief?

A 4-page personalised PDF covering: your 6-dimension career dashboard with scores and explanations, your career archetype and what it means for you specifically, a task-level breakdown of what AI will disrupt vs. amplify in your work, 1/3/5-year horizon outlooks with income trajectory, a prioritised 6-step skill roadmap, and specific AI tool and course recommendations.

What is the refund policy?

Because the report is generated and delivered instantly, we are unable to offer refunds once the PDF has been sent. If you have an issue with your report — wrong profession analysed, technical error, delivery failure — contact us and we'll sort it out immediately.
