to know.
How accurate is the analysis?

The scores are grounded in published labour market and AI capability data (Frey & Osborne, Oxford Insights, World Bank, UNDP). Your quiz answers or CV refine the baseline. Like any forward-looking model, it reflects the best available evidence — not a crystal ball. We show you the data sources so you can verify.

What do you do with my CV or LinkedIn data?

Your data is used solely to generate your report. We do not store your CV after processing, sell your data, or use it to train AI models. Your LinkedIn URL is only used to read your public profile.

How is this different from a generic AI risk score?

Most scores just look at your job title and return a number. We analyse your actual task composition, sector, experience level, and country context. Two people with the same job title in different countries can have very different risk profiles.

What exactly is in the Career Intelligence Brief?

A 4-page personalised PDF: your 6-dimension career dashboard, career archetype with explanation, task-level breakdown (what AI will disrupt vs amplify), 1/3/5-year horizon outlooks, a prioritised 6-step skill roadmap, and specific AI tool and course recommendations.

What is the refund policy?

Because the report is generated and delivered instantly, we cannot offer refunds once the PDF has been sent. If you have an issue — wrong profession, technical error, delivery failure — contact us and we'll resolve it immediately.

Yes. We're building a team and enterprise tier for workforce AI exposure analysis. Get in touch at [email protected].
